What We're About

Culture

House of Commons also known as "HOC" has an eclectic and diverse house culture and has held a reputation for being a little different from the other houses in ICC. We come from all certain aspects -- what seems to unite us as a house are our individual differences. Meals are vegan and most of the food purchased by the house is organically grown and produced. Meat is not allowed on the property despite being a vegan/vegetarian house, our members range in diet from strict vegan to omnivore. We are a motley crew, our occupations range from undergraduate/international/graduate students to working professionals. As a cooperative the house provides a social structure that cannot be found in places such as dorms and apartments. House work is shared which allows us to exist as a community and provide affordable housing. All major decisions involving the house are made using a system of modified consensus, allowing all members to participate in the decision-making process.

Amenities

House of Commons is a 21-bedroom housing cooperative located in the "West Campus" area of Austin, a few blocks from the University of Texas at Austin main campus and the Austin Community College Rio Grande campus.vThe house has a capacity of 27 residents, with 15 single occupancy and 6 double occupancy rooms.The house has a capacity of 27 residents, with 15 single occupancy and 6 double occupancy rooms and the facilities include a commercial-grade kitchen / large dining room / common areas / salt-water pool / sundeck / wireless internet with ethernet ports available in all bedrooms / laundry room with commercial washers and dryers / vehicle parking / a spacious front porch / and an herb and vegetable garden. The house also has central heating and window AC units in each bedroom.

Organization

House of Commons is a part of the University of Texas Intercooperative Council, a non-profit student housing organization responsible for the ownership and maintenance of 9 cooperative houses. ICC is owned and operated by its members, and employs a minimal office staff for administrative and maintenance tasks.
